Our Story

Providing quality equipment since 1954.

From humble beginnings.

Our founder, Randy McLahey, came over from The Netherlands in the late 1940s. Looking to escape a Europe destroyed by years of warefare and seeking new opportunities, Randy landed in New York City, bringing his love of the mountains with him. Randy quickly left the city for the countryside, but was surprised at the trouble he had finding quality gear for his long hiking trips. Seeing the potential, Randy started this company in 1954 and the rest is history. Still family owned, the company is operated by Randy's loving grandchildren.
